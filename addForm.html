The Cat Log!


It's not just a decaying lump of wood in the forest where the clouters gather to bid the souls of their human masters against secret names... It is also a place here, on the internet, right before you, that can record the names of your cats. Or your humans' - we don't judge.


Don't forget - familial that which is sensible, everyday. Particular is what is peculiar, yet dignified. Secretive names could not nearly be given justice here, with these pedestrian vowels and common consonants. Let it remain ineffable.

(Cats Only...)

Record your household, give it a name. A manse or manor or simple thing, all are welcome here, cheshire or sphinx.
